Congratulations to Intelligence Fire Robot ( IFR ) of Sun Jet Robotics Ltd. on winning 1 gold medal and 1 special prize at the 2017 Moscow international Salon of inventions and innovation technologies "Archimedes".

Our Intelligence Fire Robot was well-received at the 20th Moscow international Salon of inventions and innovation technologies "Archimedes"; held on May 15, 2017, and won 1 special prize and 1 gold medal.

With the concept of "preventing trouble at its onset and suppressing fire from origin";, Intelligence Fire Robot was developed by a collaborative team of experts from fields of disaster prevention, IoT, robotics, and system integration. IFR is capable of detecting fire in time, approaching the fire point to suppress the fire or contain its growth so as to aid the first responders. By utilizing wireless internet connection and IoT, IFR automatically sends out notifications to emergency services and improves the efficiency of disaster response and evacuation in a timely manner. It is a next generation weapon of fire protection and suppression. It is hoped that this design can be commercialized and mass-produced so as to benefit mankind and prevent fire deaths and injuries.

IFR is capable of performing security duties, automatically patrolling and detecting the protected area during ordinary times. The duo-purpose IFR protects the property with its suppression function in the event of fire and also carries out security/anti-theft tasks.

Applications of IFR include public places, factories, warehouses, health care facilities, tunnels, and historic buildings, providing complete and autonomous safety protection in places where existing fire protection equipment proves insufficient.

本公司「自動滅火機器人」參加 2017 俄羅斯發明展，獲得 1 金 1 特別獎。

「自動滅火機器人」於2017年5月15日參加「第二十屆俄羅斯阿基米德國際發明展」，深獲好評，並得到1面特別獎以及大會金牌。

「弭患於初萌，滅火於初起」，由這概念起始，並結合防災、物聯網、機器人及系統整合等各領域的專家，共同研發之「自動滅火機器人」，具備及時偵知火災，主動趨近火點，滅火制壓或延遲火勢擴大，便利消防搶救，並透過無線網路及物聯網的應用，發揮自動報案，爭取救災及避難時效，是新一代消防滅火的利器，未來期望將此設計產品化、量產化，可造福人類免於火災死傷。

本「自動滅火機器人」，平時可具備保全安防功能，自動巡邏偵測。不但可於災時防火、滅火亦可執行保全防盜功能，一機雙用。

本「自動滅火機器人」能應用於公共場所、工廠倉儲、安養照護場所、隧道、古蹟等場所，在現行消防設備防護不足之處提供完整且全自動的安全防護。
