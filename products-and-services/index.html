Intelligence Fire Robot, IFR 自動滅火機器人

As fires remain the most frequent accidents occurring in the human habitat, they continue to rage even in this modern era causing casualties and colossal property losses. The functions of current extinguishing equipment such as fire extinguishers, indoor fire hydrants and some automatic extinguisher systems are quite limited as well as insufficient.

The current invention IFR is based on the concept ‘extinguishing the fire in very early stages’ and, with a system network of modern technologies, constructed with fire protection features such as fire detection filtration, multiple signal notification, escape directional guidance, automatic approaching to the fire origin, synchronized initiation and discharging the agent , crisis management indication,firefighting information assistance. The robot also has additional security protection equipment that may carry out security missions such as patrol and surveillance, message notification, video recording and monitoring, and anti-theft measures.

火災是人類生活環境發生頻率最高的意外災害，時至今日，世界各地仍不斷發生造成人命傷亡與重大財物損失的火災。當前滅火設備：滅火器、室內消防栓及自動滅火系統等功能有侷限及不足處。

本發明「自動滅火機器人」，基於「滅火於初萌」的理念，結合現代科技系統網路，建構為具有「火警偵知過濾」、「訊息多方通報」、「導引避難逃生」、「自動趨近滅火」、「設備連動啟閉」、「危機處理提示」、「協助消防救災」等消防功能，另增附安防設備，可兼負保全「巡邏查察」、「訊息通報」、「攝影監控」及「保安防盜」等功能。
