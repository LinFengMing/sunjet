ALA Night Club Fire (2011)ALA夜店火災

發表 2017/05/08

1.Case summary 案情概要

On March 6, 2011, a fire broke out at the ALA Night Club located in Chung-hsin Street, Taichung City. The club had an area of 100m2 with elevated ceilings and a 2-storey sandwiched structure. The stage was on the ground floor, where the second floor had been turned into a seating area for people watching stage performances. The horse-shoe shaped area merely had a staircase by the stage connecting the ground and the second floor. At 1am on the night of the fire incident, a dancer was performing with fireworks on the stage and accidentally ignited the insulating foam installed on the ceiling. When the fire was at its onset, the audience was unaware of its danger, thinking it was merely a special effect. The ignited foam burned on rapidly, spreading across the ceiling within a very short amount of time and dropping combustible liquid. The flames blocked the passage way connecting the second and the ground floors, preventing most people on the second floor from escaping. In the end, this tragic incident caused 9 deaths and 13 injuries.

2011年3月6日，台中市中興街「傑克丹尼(ALA)夜店」火災，現場為約100m2挑高空間，裝潢為2樓夾層結構，舞台設於1樓，2樓供民眾觀看舞台表演之空間，作為ㄇ字型造型，僅有一條通往1樓的樓梯設在舞台邊。當日凌晨1點舞者在舞台上表演煙火秀，不慎引燃鋪設在天花板上的隔音泡綿，火災發生初期由於在場客人沒意識到火災，以為是節目特效，其後泡棉燃燒快速，在短時間內就擴大延燒整個天花板並滴落燃燒可燃液體，火勢阻隔2樓通往1樓的通道，2樓多數民眾不及逃生，最後造成9人死亡13人受傷的悲劇。

2.Reflection 省思

The Fire Services Act requires installation of fire protection equipment in various types of occupancies according to their use, area, and number of floors. Usually, smaller commercial premises are merely required to install fire extinguishers or indoor fire hydrants. Nevertheless, such passive fire protection equipment needs to be manually and correctly operated and, therefore, its performance is often influenced by factors such as business operation environment and regular staff trainings. If a tool can be developed to provide mobile protection, automatically locating the fire point, and extinguishing the fire at its onset, commercial premises of all scales shall be better protected.

消防法規係依各類場所使用用途、面積、樓層而要求設置消防設備，小型營業場所通常僅要求設滅火器或室內消防栓，但這種被動式需人們正確操作使用的消防設備，常因營業場所環境、員工平日組訓等因素，影響其校能發揮，若能開發一種移動式自動尋找火點於火災初起即予撲滅的工具，將有助於防護各類大型、中型、小型營業場所。
