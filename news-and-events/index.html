Hsindian Nursing Home Fire (2016)新店安養院火災

發表 2017/05/09

Case summary 案情概要

At 7am on July 6, 2016, a fire suddenly broke out at the LOHAS Senior Nursing Center, located on the 8th floor of a premise on Chungcheng Road, Hsiandian District. The fire point was in Room #5 and initially identified as an electrical fire. As most occupants in the nursing home were unable to take care of themselves in daily life or to move freely, including the elderly, people with dementia, immobile people, or cancer patients who need post-surgery care, they need other people’s assistance to evacuate in the event of fire. The center merely employed four nurses, three of whom were of foreign nationalities. It did not have sufficient man power and failed to properly respond to the incident. When the fire occurred, there was no way to assist the occupants to evacuate effectively. In addition, the air-conditioning was not switched off, so thick smoke spread rapidly, preventing firefighters on the 8th floor from responding efficiently and ending up causing 6 deaths, 3 of whom were residents of Room #5 where the fire started, as well as 28 additional injuries.

2016年7月6日新店區中正路1家位於8樓的「樂活老人照護中心」，上午7時突然竄出火苗，起火點在5號房間，初步判斷是電器所引起火災。由於照護中心大部分都是生活無法自理、行動不便的長者、失智老人，或是癱瘓、癌症術後需調養的病患，火災發生就必須仰賴他人協助避難，該中心僅配置4名看護(其中3名外籍看護)，人力不足且應變作為不足，當火災發生時，根本無法協助收容人員進行有效避難，且因空調未關閉，濃煙擴散快速，另8樓的位置消防人員搶救困難，最終造成住民6死28傷(其中3名死者住起火的5號病房)。

Hsinwu Bowling Alley Fire (2015)新屋保齡球館火災

發表 2017/05/09

Case summary 案情概要

At 2am on January 20, 2015, a fire broke out at the Hsinwu Bowling Alley on Chunghsing North Road in Taoyuan City. The fire scene was a large 2-storey steel-frame construction covered with iron sheet. With an area of 900 m2 on each floor, the ground floor was used as a swimming pool whereas the second a bowling alley. According to the regulations, the construction was equipped with fire extinguishers, indicating devices, emergency lights, indoor fire hydrants, automatic fire alarms, and emergency broadcasting speakers and an automatic sprinkler system was installed in the bowling alley on the second floor. The fire started after hours in the middle of night with a fire origin at an electrical room where no fire suppression system was installed. The fire spread to the bowling alley on the second floor and was growing full force. The automatic sprinkler system installed did not activate and eventually a flashover occurred, taking the lives of six responders.

2015年1月20日2時，桃園市新屋區中興北路「新屋保齡球館」發生火災，現場為大型二層鋼骨鐵皮結構建築物，單層面積約900m2，一層作為游泳池、二層作為保齡球館使用，依規定設有滅火器、標示設備、緊急照明燈、室內消防栓、火警自動警報設備、緊急廣播設備等，在二樓保齡球遊戲區自設自動撒水設備。火災發生於半夜非營業時間，起火點在電氣機房燃燒，機房未設滅火設備，火勢蔓延擴大至二樓保齡球球道區全面燃燒，自設之自動撒水系統並未發揮作用，終至產生閃燃，導致6名救災人員犧牲。

Single Family House Fire in Yuanlin District, Changhua County (2014)彰化員林麵包店透天厝火災

發表 2017/05/09

Case summary 案情概要

At 5am on April 14, 2014, a fire broke out at a bakery in Yuanlin District, Changhua County. The incident took place in a row house constructed with wood partition walls. The store front and a kitchen were on the ground floor with an iron roller door at the entrance. The second floor was used as a residence with illegally installed iron sheet construction on the rooftop. A signboard was attached to windows from the second floor upward and blocked the escape route. At 5am that morning, a fire started in the rear bakery oven at the kitchen located on the ground floor. As the occupants were fast asleep upstairs, they woke up finding the stairs and hallways already filled with thick smoke, which made it impossible to escape through the stairs. The street front windows on the second floor were blocked by the bakery’s own signboard and the iron roller door couldn’t be opened due to power outage. Unable to escape, five family members of three generations lost their lives.

2014年4月14日清晨5時彰化縣員林鎮一家麵包烘焙坊發生火警，現場為一連棟透天厝建築，木板隔間，1樓做為店鋪及廚房使用，出入口為鐵捲門構造，2樓作為住家使用，頂樓設有鐵皮違建，2樓以上向外窗戶設有招牌，阻擋逃生。當日凌晨5點廚房發生火災，起火點在1樓後側烘培坊烤箱，樓上民眾正在熟睡，等到察覺時樓梯通道都已濃煙密布，無法下樓避難，2樓面街窗戶因自家商店廣告封住，且1樓鐵捲門也因斷電而無法開啟，無法逃生，導致一家三代五口罹難。

Taishan Furniture Warehouse Fire (2013)泰山家具倉庫火災

發表 2017/05/09

Case summary 案情概要

At 1pm on July 6, 2013, a fire broke out at Reeta International CO., LTD. on Chung Kang West Road, Taishan District, New Taipei City. The fire scene took place in a furniture warehouse constructed with iron sheet. Taking up 600 Ping in area, the warehouse stored a large amount of combustible furniture and goods and was left unattended in ordinary times. According to related regulations, the warehouse should install equipment such as automatic fire alarms, indoor fire hydrants and fire extinguishers. However, if no one is around when the fire breaks out, it will all be in vain. The fire burned through a surface area of 300 Ping and even involved 3 adjacent structures that belonged to a metal factory. The fire was contained at 3pm. During the response, the sandwiched level on the second floor appeared to have a flashover and the floor collapsed, causing two firefighters to die in line of duty.

2013年7月6日13時，位於新北市泰山區中港西路的「利塔國際有限公司」發生火災，火場是鐵皮搭蓋家具倉庫，占地600坪，內部放置大量可燃家具貨物，平時並無人留守管理，該倉庫使用用途及面積依法規應設有自動警報設備及室內消防栓、滅火器等設備但火災發生時，無人操作也無濟於事，燃燒面積約300坪，延燒到旁邊的金屬工廠等3棟建築，火勢在下午3時控制。搶救過程中，疑似2樓夾層發生閃燃現象，現場發生倒塌，造成2名消防人員殉職。

Welcome Restaurant Fire (1995)威爾康西餐廳火災

發表 2017/05/08

Case Summary 案情概要

At 7pm on February 15, 1995, the gas stove top located adjacent to the staircase on the ground floor of the Welcome Restaurant on Chungkang Road, Taichung City, was ignited. The fire swallowed the surrounding combustibles rapidly, spreading across the ground floor and then to the second floor via the staircase.

The western style restaurant was an illegally built 2-storey iron sheet structure which had been refurbished to be turned into a restaurant. There were fire extinguishers, but no other fire protection and safety equipment was found. When the fire broke out, customers on the ground floor ran out through the main entrance. The restaurant employees failed to suppress the fire effectively, nor did they notify every occupant to evacuate. Therefore, many people didn’t escape in time. After the fire was extinguished, 7 bodies were found in the kitchen at rear on the ground floor and 57 bodies on the second floor. This incident is the single most deadly fire in Taiwan.

1995年2月15日晚上7點，台中市中港路威爾康西餐廳一樓樓梯旁櫃台瓦斯爐起火，引燃周邊可燃材料，火勢快速在一樓蔓延，並且循樓梯向二樓延燒。該西餐廳是一棟兩層樓鐵皮違建，改裝為餐廳使用，除滅火器外，並無其他消防安全設備，當火災發生時，一樓顧客倉促從大門逃出，店內員工未能有效撲滅火災，且未及時通報所有人員立即逃生，造成多人逃生不及，火災撲滅後一樓後方廚房發現7具屍體、二樓發現57具屍體，本案為台灣地區單一火災死傷人數最多的重大火災。

ALA Night Club Fire (2011)ALA夜店火災

發表 2017/05/08

Case summary 案情概要

On March 6, 2011, a fire broke out at the ALA Night Club located in Chung-hsin Street, Taichung City. The club had an area of 100 m2 with elevated ceilings and a 2-storey sandwiched structure. The stage was on the ground floor, where the second floor had been turned into a seating area for people watching stage performances. The horse-shoe shaped area merely had a staircase by the stage connecting the ground and the second floor. At 1am on the night of the fire incident, a dancer was performing with fireworks on the stage and accidentally ignited the insulating foam installed on the ceiling. When the fire was at its onset, the audience was unaware of its danger, thinking it was merely a special effect. The ignited foam burned on rapidly, spreading across the ceiling within a very short amount of time and dropping combustible liquid. The flames blocked the passage way connecting the second and the ground floors, preventing most people on the second floor from escaping. In the end, this tragic incident caused 9 deaths and 13 injuries.

2011年3月6日，台中市中興街「傑克丹尼(ALA)夜店」火災，現場為約100m2挑高空間，裝潢為2樓夾層結構，舞台設於1樓，2樓供民眾觀看舞台表演之空間，作為ㄇ字型造型，僅有一條通往1樓的樓梯設在舞台邊。當日凌晨1點舞者在舞台上表演煙火秀，不慎引燃鋪設在天花板上的隔音泡綿，火災發生初期由於在場客人沒意識到火災，以為是節目特效，其後泡棉燃燒快速，在短時間內就擴大延燒整個天花板並滴落燃燒可燃液體，火勢阻隔2樓通往1樓的通道，2樓多數民眾不及逃生，最後造成9人死亡13人受傷的悲劇。
