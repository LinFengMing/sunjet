Welcome Restaurant Fire (1995)威爾康西餐廳火災

發表 2017/05/08

1.Case Summary 案情概要

At 7pm on February 15, 1995, the gas stove top located adjacent to the staircase on the ground floor of the Welcome Restaurant on Chungkang Road, Taichung City, was ignited. The fire swallowed the surrounding combustibles rapidly, spreading across the ground floor and then to the second floor via the staircase.

The western style restaurant was an illegally built 2-storey iron sheet structure which had been refurbished to be turned into a restaurant. There were fire extinguishers, but no other fire protection and safety equipment was found. When the fire broke out, customers on the ground floor ran out through the main entrance. The restaurant employees failed to suppress the fire effectively, nor did they notify every occupant to evacuate. Therefore, many people didn’t escape in time. After the fire was extinguished, 7 bodies were found in the kitchen at rear on the ground floor and 57 bodies on the second floor. This incident is the single most deadly fire in Taiwan.

1995年2月15日晚上7點，台中市中港路威爾康西餐廳一樓樓梯旁櫃台瓦斯爐起火，引燃周邊可燃材料，火勢快速在一樓蔓延，並且循樓梯向二樓延燒。該西餐廳是一棟兩層樓鐵皮違建，改裝為餐廳使用，除滅火器外，並無其他消防安全設備，當火災發生時，一樓顧客倉促從大門逃出，店內員工未能有效撲滅火災，且未及時通報所有人員立即逃生，造成多人逃生不及，火災撲滅後一樓後方廚房發現7具屍體、二樓發現57具屍體，本案為台灣地區單一火災死傷人數最多的重大火災。

2.Reflection 省思

Fire extinguishers and indoor fire hydrants are passive fire protection equipment that require proper manual operation. When fire suddenly breaks out, people might fail to operate the equipment correctly due to panic and anxiety and thereby allow the fire to grow uncontrollably. If there is an autonomous fire protection system capable of detecting the fire in its very early stages, approaching the fire rapidly, and extinguishing the fire of its own accord, the fire will not grow extendedly and end up causing severe casualties and property losses. Thus, safety of various types of public and commercial premises shall be improved.

滅火器、室內消防栓為被動式需人們正確操作的消防設備，當火災突發，人們在驚慌、緊張情緒，可能無法正確操作，有效滅火，導致火勢擴大，如能開發極早偵知，快速趨近，自動滅火之消防設備，滅火於初起，即不會造成擴大燃燒導致重大傷亡與財物損失，各類公共營業場所當可進一步提升其安全。
