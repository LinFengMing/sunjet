Taishan Furniture Warehouse Fire (2013)泰山家具倉庫火災

發表 2017/05/09

1.Case summary 案情概要

At 1pm on July 6, 2013, a fire broke out at Reeta International CO., LTD. on Chung Kang West Road, Taishan District, New Taipei City. The fire scene took place in a furniture warehouse constructed with iron sheet. Taking up 600 Ping in area, the warehouse stored a large amount of combustible furniture and goods and was left unattended in ordinary times. According to related regulations, the warehouse should install equipment such as automatic fire alarms, indoor fire hydrants and fire extinguishers. However, if no one is around when the fire breaks out, it will all be in vain. The fire burned through a surface area of 300 Ping and even involved 3 adjacent structures that belonged to a metal factory. The fire was contained at 3pm. During the response, the sandwiched level on the second floor appeared to have a flashover and the floor collapsed, causing two firefighters to die in line of duty.

2013年7月6日13時，位於新北市泰山區中港西路的「利塔國際有限公司」發生火災，火場是鐵皮搭蓋家具倉庫，占地600坪，內部放置大量可燃家具貨物，平時並無人留守管理，該倉庫使用用途及面積依法規應設有自動警報設備及室內消防栓、滅火器等設備但火災發生時，無人操作也無濟於事，燃燒面積約300坪，延燒到旁邊的金屬工廠等3棟建築，火勢在下午3時控制。搶救過程中，疑似2樓夾層發生閃燃現象，現場發生倒塌，造成2名消防人員殉職。

2.Reflection 省思

When the first responders arrived at the fire scene, what troubled them the most was an uncertain situation where there is little known regarding the fire point, extent of burn, trapped victims and their locations. Therefore, there was need to enter the fire scene to conduct searches. There were no occupants in the Taishan Furniture Warehouse on holidays. The fire smoke was reported by a neighbor. Apparently, it had been burning for a long time. As the warehouse was huge and filled with thick smoke, firefighters had to respond to the incident in the hazardous environment and tragedy happened when the racks collapsed at flashover. If an autonomous mobile suppression equipment can be developed to provide protection for factories and warehouses, search for the location of fire point, approach and suppress the fire while notifying 119 emergency services, the firefighting crew will only need to conduct the overhaul operation. The fire will not have the chance to grow uncontrollably or even jeopardize the safety of firefighters.

消防救災人員到達火場最困擾的是狀況不明，不知火點在何處？燃燒範圍多大？是否有人被困或在何處被困？故需進入火場搜索。本案泰山家具倉庫，假日無人看守，火災煙霧被鄰居發現報案，顯然燃燒已久，倉庫又大，內部都是濃煙，消防人員在危險環境中搶救，遇閃燃貨架倒塌，造成不幸。若能開發一種移動式自動滅火設備，作為工廠、倉庫安全防護，火點產生即自動尋找、趨近、滅火，同時通報119。在消防人員到達之前就能撲滅或壓制火勢，消防人員到達現場後，僅需進行殘火處理，不至於火勢擴大到不可收拾的地步，甚至危及消防人員的安全。
